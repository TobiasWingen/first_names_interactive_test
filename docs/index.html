Stereotypes about Names

This website presents stereotypic ratings of first names, as judged by German participants under laboratory conditions. For detailed information about the methodology, please refer to Speckmann and Wingen (2023) . It is important to note that these ratings were provided by a non-representative sample of students and do not reflect actual attributes of individuals bearing these names.
